This book is dedicated to my grandmother, who passed away in late October 2018. It still hurts when I think about her, but it seems like it is getting a bit better over time. There are days when I would stare into nothingness, and it crushes me inside. But I also learn to move on, and continue to live my life. Things seem to get back to normal as time goes by. And this book is my attempt to capture the pain inside me as I grieve the death of my grandmother.
