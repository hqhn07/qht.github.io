Quynh Tran makes prints, artist's books and other works on paper. Her work has been exhibited in Denton (TX), Rockport (TX), Sacramento (CA), Lincoln (NE), Omaha (NE), Las Vegas (NV), Carson City (NV), Reno (NV) and Hanoi (Vietnam).

Quynh Tran was born in Vietnam and came to the U.S. for her undergraduate study. She earned her B.F.A in Printmaking & B.A in Advertising from the University of North Texas at Denton. Quynh Tran finished her M.F.A in Studio Arts–Concentration in Printmaking & Book Arts at the University of Nevada, Reno in May 2017. Currently, she is living and working in Chapel Hill, North Carolina.
